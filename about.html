About ELBEX

ELBEX Couriers Private Limited — Shipping made simpler

ELBEX is an emerging, full-service courier, cargo & logistics provider with a robust nationwide footprint. We combine technology, operations excellence, and a customer-first approach to deliver efficient distribution and delivery solutions.

Comprehensive Distribution: End-to-end logistics from pickup to delivery, domestic and international.
Technology-driven: We use e-TABS (Electronic Tracking, Auditing & Billing System) — a web-based track & trace platform for real-time consignment updates.
Customer first: Seamless experience, flexible service options and strong local presence.

ELBEX Overview

ELBEX Couriers Private Limited (ECPL) has grown steadily to be one of India's trusted courier and cargo companies. Prepared to face modern logistics challenges, ELBEX provides flexible, swift and reliable services ensuring stakeholders a competitive edge.

One of our core strengths is leveraging cutting-edge, path-breaking technology. Our in-house e-TABS (Electronic, Tracking, Auditing & Billing System) delivers user-friendly, web-based track & trace capabilities providing real-time updates on consignment movement.

e-TABS — Track & Trace

Real-time consignment visibility, auditing, billing automation and streamlined workflows — simplifying operations for franchisees and customers alike.
